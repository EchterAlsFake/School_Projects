Nadya DOROFEEVA (ukr. Надія Дорофєєва) is a popular singer famous for her trendy and modern pop music.

Biography

Nadya Dorofeeva was born on 21 April 1990 in Simferopol, Ukraine. From a young age, Nadya was engaged in music and dancing. In 2002, at the age of 12, Nadya became regional champion in ballroom dancing, being crowned the prize of best ballroom dancer in her native Crimea. In 2005, after finishing the ninth class, Nadya moved to Moscow, where she started studying at the Moscow State Art and Cultural University for a degree in singing. She eventually moved to Moscow to study singing at the university. Later on she won several prices. However, her career breakthrough was with Vremya i Steklo. Potap, a ukrainian producer searched for a new female vocalist for his project. Nadya won the contest and together with Oleksiy Zavgorodniy they created music. For this purpose she also moved back to Kyiv. They weren't succesful until 2015 where the Single "Imya 505" got a breakthrough in Russia and is as of now the most listened song. They were active from 2010-2020. After that they both started with their individual careers.
